Golden Labrador divided into age categories:

From birth to six months: Meet the Golden Labrador puppy—a fluffy tornado of mischief and cuteness! At this age, they're experts in chewing socks and napping on your lap. They're still learning the ropes, often mistaking slippers for chew toys and puddles for swimming pools.

From six months to one year: The adolescent Golden Labrador is like a teenager—full of boundless energy and questionable decisions! They've outgrown some puppy habits but are still mastering commands like "sit" and "stay." Beware of their newfound enthusiasm for digging and chasing squirrels.

From one year to adulthood (around three years): Now in their prime, the Golden Labrador is a social butterfly. They excel in games of fetch and have a knack for stealing hearts with their soulful eyes. They may occasionally pretend not to hear commands, especially when distracted by a tantalizing smell or an intriguing mud puddle.

Adult Golden Labrador: Ah, the mature Golden Labrador—a loyal companion and expert in stealing food when no one's looking. They've perfected the art of giving guilt-inducing stares for extra treats and have a knack for finding the coziest spots on the couch. They still have bursts of puppy-like energy but also enjoy long naps and being the center of attention at family gatherings.
